Stitch App

A project about music

Stitch is a music app that is powered by good shows and the people that attend them. It aims to create a rich, dynamic and interactive crowdsourced video experience for amateur concert filmers. By stitching together multiple videos, all with unique angles and perspective, Stitch produces dynamic, engaging video worth sharing.
