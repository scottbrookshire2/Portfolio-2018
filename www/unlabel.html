Unlabel App

Building the the Unlabel App

Our primary user need was centered around helping people find fun things to do. We reshaped our event discovery experience across our ecosystem to bring more life, levity and fun to the app. To become more of a truset source we elevated the emphasis on city and integreated editorial and third party content. For this effort, my role was to redesign and systematize the visual language accross the app. I also led the product design effort for the feed, on-boarding, profiles, likes tab, and event listings. I worked with a small team of designers and developers. Special thanks to Dave Obrien, Sara Kremer, Marina Vu, Sadie Williams, and Lumen Bigott for collaborating on the experience, illustrations, city icones and UI icons.

  • Roles
  • Product designer
  • UX architect

The challenge

We wanted to create a new consumer-centric visual system that balances the Eventbrite brand with the excitment of discovering something fun to do.
