Unlabel App

Building the the Unlabel app

Unlabel is a marketplace comprised of the most innovative independent brands, with products curated and reviewed by a community of digital creatives. Their primary user need is centered around discovering interesting trends and boutiques. When creating the design system I wanted to full incorporat the brand's minimalist aesthetic — content over controls.

  • Roles
  • Product Designer

The Challenge

To feel more editorial, we used a lot of white space, a geometric typeface, and minimal color. Less is more.
