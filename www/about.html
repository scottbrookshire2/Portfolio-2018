Hi, my name is Scott

This is the story all about how my life got flipped turned upside down.

I love design. My exposure to the art world started with watercolors and pastels, but I now specialize in UI, UX, motion graphics, prototyping, web design, identity work, and logo design.

I enjoy creating rich user experiences that feel both immersive and effortless. When designing I pay close attention to my client's brand. I believe that concept is paramount and harmony should always be adhered to. A strong ideation process should always play a key role in every design decision. But before anything, I always start on paper. Whether it's logo sketches or wireframes, I utilize the tried and true art of pen and paper.
