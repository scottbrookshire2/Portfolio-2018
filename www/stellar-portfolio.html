Stellar

A project about music synthesis

Stellar is an interactive synthesizer made for browsers (currently in progress). Users click different regions on the screen to play a sound. When two or more regions are clicked the sounds are arpeggiated. Taking inspiration from the night sky, users end goal produces a customized constellation. Made in collaboration with Ryan Jones, Emilee Serafine, and Sam Evans.
