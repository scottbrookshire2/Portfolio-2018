Lacuna

A project about the mind

Lacuna is a group project created for school. Taking inspiration from the film [ital]Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind[end ital], Lacuna is a memory modification company on the frontier of mental mapping. They seek to unburden, protect, and cherish the mind of man through precisely-tailored memory manipulation. Made in collaboration with Emilee Serafine, Theresa Ma, and Andrew Durano.
