Enrich

A project about school

Enrich is a program that provides technology access to students in need. It starts with everyday people donating their leftover data. This data then fuels tablets that have internet access. Students can then check out tablets, each of which comes preinstalled with the Enrich app. Through Enrich, students can read interactive literature, receive support from teachers, and connect with other students outside of the classroom.
